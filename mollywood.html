Malayalam Film Industry

Manjummel Boys (2024)

Manjummel Boys is a 2024 Malayalam-language biographical survival thriller film directed by Chidambaram. The story revolves around a group of friends from Manjummel, Kochi, who embark on a vacation to Kodaikanal. Their adventure takes a perilous turn when one of them, Subhash, falls into the Guna Caves. The film captures their struggle to rescue him and the challenges they face. With a stellar cast including Soubin Shahir, Sreenath Bhasi, and Balu Varghese, the film received critical acclaim for its technical aspects, performances, and direction. It became the highest-grossing Malayalam film, earning over ₹200 crore.

2018 (2023)

The 2018 Malayalam movie, titled "2018" is a survival disaster film based on the 2018 Kerala floods. Directed by Jude Anthany Joseph, the film features an ensemble cast including Kunchacko Boban, Tovino Thomas, Asif Ali, and Vineeth Sreenivasan. The story revolves around various characters from different walks of life who come together to survive the catastrophic floods. The film received positive reviews and was a commercial success, grossing ₹177 crore at the box office .

The Goat Life (2024)

The Goat Life" (2024), also known as "Aadujeevitham," is a Malayalam-language survival drama film directed by Blessy. Based on Benyamin's novel, it tells the harrowing true story of Najeeb, a Malayali immigrant laborer in Saudi Arabia. Trapped in the desert, Najeeb endures brutal conditions as a goat herder. The film stars Prithviraj Sukumaran, Amala Paul, and Jimmy Jean-Louis, with music by A. R. Rahman. It received critical acclaim for its powerful storytelling and performances, grossing over ₹158 crore at the box office

Aavesham (2024)

Aavesham" (2024) is a Malayalam-language action comedy film directed by Jithu Madhavan. The film stars Fahadh Faasil, Hipzster, Mithun Jai Shankar, Roshan Shanavas, and Sajin Gopu. The story follows three youngsters in Bengaluru who befriend a local gangster, Ranga, to seek revenge on their college bully. The film received critical acclaim for its direction, performances, action sequences, and technical aspects. It became a blockbuster, grossing over ₹154 crore against a budget of ₹30 crore.

Marco (2024)

Marco" (2024) is a Malayalam-language neo-noir action thriller directed by Haneef Adeni. The film stars Unni Mukundan as Marco, a ruthless criminal seeking revenge for his blind foster brother Victor's brutal death. The plot follows Marco's bloody rampage against those responsible, including the gold smuggling syndicate led by Tony Issac. The film features Siddique, Jagadish, Kabir Duhan Singh, and others in supporting roles. With music by Ravi Basrur, "Marco" was a massive commercial success, grossing ₹155 crore and becoming the highest-grossing A-rated Malayalam film of all time.

Pulimurugan (2016)

Pulimurugan" (2016) is a Malayalam-language epic action thriller directed by Vysakh. The film stars Mohanlal as Murugan, a skilled hunter who protects his village from man-eating tigers. After losing his parents to tiger attacks, Murugan becomes a fearless protector, earning the nickname Pulimurugan. The film also features Jagapati Babu, Kamalinee Mukherjee, and Lal. With stunning action sequences and a gripping storyline, "Pulimurugan" became the first Malayalam film to earn over ₹100 crore, setting several box office records

Premalu (2024)

Premalu" (2024) is a Malayalam-language romantic comedy directed by Girish A. D. The film stars Naslen K. Gafoor and Mamitha Baiju, alongside Sangeeth Prathap, Akhila Bhargavan, Shyam Mohan, Meenakshi Raveendran, Mathew Thomas, and Althaf Salim. The story follows Sachin Santhosh, a graduate from Kerala, who plans to move to the UK but ends up in Hyderabad for a GATE course. There, he meets and falls in love with Reenu Roy, an IT company employee. The film received critical acclaim for its performances, script, soundtrack, direction, and humor, grossing ₹136.25 crore at the box office

Lucifer (2019)

"Lucifer" (2019) is a Malayalam-language political action thriller directed by Prithviraj Sukumaran. The film stars Mohanlal as Stephen Nedumpally, a charismatic and enigmatic political leader. The plot revolves around the power struggle following the death of the state's Chief Minister, with Stephen emerging as a key player. The film also features Vivek Oberoi, Manju Warrier, and Tovino Thomas in pivotal roles. With its gripping storyline, powerful performances, and high-octane action sequences, "Lucifer" became a massive commercial success, grossing over ₹200 crore at the box office.

Arm (2024)

ARM" (2024), also known as "Ajayante Randam Moshanam," is a Malayalam-language action-adventure film directed by Jithin Laal. The film stars Tovino Thomas in triple roles, alongside Krithi Shetty, Basil Joseph, and Aishwarya Rajesh. Set across three time periods—1900, 1950, and 1990—the story follows three generations of heroes, Maniyan, Kunjikelu, and Ajayan, as they protect the sacred treasure, Chyothivilakku. The film received positive reviews for its performances, direction, and visual effects, grossing ₹106.75 crore at the box office

Guruvayoor Ambalanadayil (2024)

Guruvayoor Ambalanadayil" (2024) is a Malayalam-language comedy-drama directed by Vipin Das and written by Deepu Pradeep. The film stars Prithviraj Sukumaran, Basil Joseph, Nikhila Vimal, and Anaswara Rajan. The story follows Vinu Ramachandran, a young executive in Dubai, who forms a close bond with his fiancée's brother, Anandan. Chaos ensues when they discover that Vinu's ex-girlfriend and Anandan's wife are the same person, leading to a series of comedic and dramatic events. The film received positive reviews and became a blockbuster, grossing ₹90.25 crore at the box office
